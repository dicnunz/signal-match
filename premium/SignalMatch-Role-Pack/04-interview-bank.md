# Interview Bank

Practice these out loud using STAR.

## Data To Decision

Tell me about a time you turned reporting or analysis into a recommendation.

## Workflow Repair

Describe a broken process you improved. What was wrong before, what changed,
and what happened after?

## Cross-Functional Alignment

Tell me about a time multiple teams wanted different things. How did you move
the work forward?

## Customer Risk

Describe a time you spotted churn, adoption, or onboarding risk early. What did
you see, and what action followed?

## Tool Judgment

Tell me about a tool or system you used heavily. What did it enable, and where
did it slow the team down?

## Metric Ownership

What KPI or operating metric have you watched most closely? Why that one?
