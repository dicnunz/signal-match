# 7-Day Application Sprint

## Day 1

- choose one role
- paste the job description into SignalMatch
- mark matched and missing signals

## Day 2

- fill the proof harvester
- collect real metrics, scope, and stakeholder details

## Day 3

- rewrite the summary
- rewrite the top four bullets

## Day 4

- tailor the resume header
- tailor the skills section
- remove irrelevant bullets

## Day 5

- rehearse the interview bank
- write three STAR stories

## Day 6

- submit the application
- save the role in the targeting sheet
- draft a short LinkedIn note if needed

## Day 7

- review what felt weak
- improve the next application from those gaps
