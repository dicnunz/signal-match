# Proof Harvester

Before you rewrite anything, answer these prompts for each experience entry.

## Scope

- How many accounts, users, customers, projects, or requests were involved?
- How often did this work happen?
- Who looked at the output?
- What team or function did this support?

## Business Impact

- What improved after your work?
- What got faster?
- What got cleaner?
- What got more accurate?
- What risk went down?
- What decision became easier?

## Process

- What was broken before?
- What exactly did you change?
- What tool, workflow, report, or system did you touch?
- Which stakeholders did you have to align?

## Evidence Starters

Use these only if true:

- `supported 65 accounts`
- `reduced manual reporting time`
- `built weekly dashboard used by leadership`
- `standardized onboarding handoff`
- `flagged churn risk early`
- `improved data hygiene in Salesforce`

## Rule

If you cannot prove a claim in an interview, do not add it to the resume.
