# Bullet Rewrite Rubric

Use this format:

`Strong verb + object + scope + business effect`

## Replace weak openings

- `Worked with` -> `Partnered with`
- `Helped with` -> `Supported`
- `Was responsible for` -> `Owned`
- `Did reporting for` -> `Built reporting for`
- `Handled` -> `Managed` or `Owned`

## Keep bullets tight

- one sentence
- one main idea
- no filler adjectives
- no vague claims like `various`, `several`, `many` unless you have to

## Better patterns

Instead of:

`Worked with the customer success team on onboarding.`

Use:

`Partnered with customer success leadership to tighten onboarding handoff workflows and improve visibility into completion risk.`

Instead of:

`Created reports for leadership.`

Use:

`Built weekly retention and onboarding reports used by leadership to review customer health and escalation risk.`

## Final check

Before keeping a bullet, ask:

- Is the verb strong?
- Is the object clear?
- Is there any scope or frequency?
- Is there a business reason this matters?
- Can I defend this live?
