# SignalMatch Launch Plan

## Goal

Ship SignalMatch as a public free scan with a paid `$19` role pack and a later
`$49` sprint upsell.

## Positioning

SignalMatch is for job seekers who want stronger applications without fake AI
fluff.

Core line:

`Beat the AI screen without faking anything.`

## Funnel

1. Public landing page and free scan.
2. CTA into the paid role pack.
3. Follow-up sprint upgrade for urgent candidates.

## Immediate tasks

1. Finish the Gumroad draft and capture the public product URL.
2. Drop that URL into `lib/site.ts`.
3. Republish the site.
4. Post the first launch messages.

## Distribution

- LinkedIn first
- X second
- direct messages to job-seeker friends and recent grads third

## Why this can work

- urgent buyer pain
- simple first product
- honest positioning
- zero paid API cost
- easy free-to-paid ladder
